APSC Preparation Program

Comprehensive coaching for Assam Public Service Commission examinations

About the Program

Our APSC Preparation Program is meticulously designed to help aspiring candidates excel in Assam Public Service Commission examinations. With experienced faculty, comprehensive study materials, and regular assessments, we provide the perfect environment for success.

The program covers all aspects of APSC examinations including Prelims, Mains, and Interview preparation. We focus on building strong fundamentals, developing analytical skills, and providing extensive practice through mock tests and previous year question analysis.

Program Benefits

Expert Faculty

Learn from experienced teachers and APSC qualified professionals who understand the examination pattern thoroughly.

Comprehensive Study Material

Access to well-researched and updated study materials covering entire syllabus with topic-wise notes and practice questions.

Regular Mock Tests

Weekly mock tests simulating actual APSC exam conditions with detailed performance analysis and feedback.

Interview Preparation

Dedicated sessions for personality development and mock interviews to build confidence and communication skills.

Current Affairs Updates

Daily current affairs classes focusing on Assam, Northeast India, and national events relevant to APSC.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the APSC preparation course?

The complete APSC package typically runs for 12-18 months, covering Prelims, Mains, and Interview preparation. Shorter focused courses are also available for specific stages.

What are the eligibility criteria for enrollment?

Candidates must have a graduate degree from a recognized university. We welcome students at all stages of preparation, from beginners to those appearing for second or third attempts.

Are study materials provided?

Yes, comprehensive study materials including textbooks, current affairs magazines, test series, and online resources are provided to all enrolled students.

What is the class schedule?

Classes are conducted 5-6 days a week with both morning and evening batches available. Weekend batches are also available for working professionals.

What is the success rate of your students?

We are proud of our 95% success rate in APSC examinations. Many of our students have secured top ranks in various services.
