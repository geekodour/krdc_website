About Koch Rajbongshi Development Council

The Koch Rajbongshi Development Council is committed to the educational and socio-economic advancement of the Koch Rajbongshi community in Assam. Through our comprehensive programs and dedicated faculty, we strive to create opportunities for growth, learning, and development.

Our institution serves as a beacon of hope and progress, offering pathways to success through competitive exam preparation, vocational training, and holistic educational support.
