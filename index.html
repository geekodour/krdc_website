About Koch Rajbongshi Development Council (KRDC)

The Koch Rajbongshi Development Council (KRDC) is a statutory body of the Government of Assam dedicated to the socio-economic, educational, and cultural development of the Koch Rajbongshi community. It focuses on empowering youth through education, skill development, livelihood initiatives, and capacity building, with special attention to students from rural and economically disadvantaged backgrounds. The Council promotes academic support, vocational training, competitive exam coaching, and scholarship-linked programmes to enable higher education and government employment. KRDC works through district-level outreach and partnerships to improve access to quality education and guidance.
