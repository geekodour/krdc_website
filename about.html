About Us

Dedicated to the advancement and empowerment of the Koch Rajbongshi community through quality education

Our Mission

To empower the Koch Rajbongshi community through accessible, quality education and comprehensive skill development programs. We are committed to creating pathways for academic excellence, competitive examination success, and sustainable livelihood opportunities.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution for the Koch Rajbongshi community, fostering a generation of skilled, knowledgeable, and empowered individuals who contribute meaningfully to society while preserving and celebrating our rich cultural heritage.

Our Story

The Koch Rajbongshi Development Council was established with a singular vision: to create educational opportunities that empower the Koch Rajbongshi community of Assam. Recognizing the need for targeted educational support and skill development, our founders brought together educators, community leaders, and professionals dedicated to this cause.

Over the years, we have grown from a small initiative to a comprehensive educational institution offering programs ranging from competitive exam coaching to vocational training. Our success is measured not just in examination results, but in the transformed lives of our students and their families.

Today, we stand as a beacon of hope and progress, committed to excellence in education while staying rooted in our cultural values. Our programs are designed to meet the evolving needs of our community, preparing students for both traditional competitive examinations and modern career opportunities.
